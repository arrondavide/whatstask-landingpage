## The Cheat Codes (What Actually Works)

### 1. Template SEO (ClickUp's Secret)
ClickUp ranks for thousands of keywords with templates:
- "marketing campaign template"
- "sprint planning template"
- "project timeline template"

**Each template = 1 SEO page**

Create 100 templates = 100 ranking opportunities

### 2. Comparison Pages (Aggressive but Works)
Your comparison pages ARE working. Double down:
- Add more detail
- Add feature tables
- Add migration guides
- Create "Why teams switch from X" posts

### 3. Long-Tail Content
Instead of fighting for "project management software":
- "telegram task management app"
- "simple project management for small teams"
- "free ai project management tool"
- "project management without complexity"

### 4. Founder-Led Content
People trust people more than brands:
- Write from founder perspective
- Share building journey
- Be opinionated
- Use Twitter/LinkedIn

### 5. Community Over Ads
Monday spends $200M/year on ads. You can't compete.
But you can:
- Build Telegram community
- Engage on Reddit/Twitter
- Do podcast interviews
- Guest post
- Partner with influencers

## What NOT to Do

### Don't Try to Match Feature Count
ClickUp has 15 views. You have 4. **That's your advantage.**

### Don't Spread Too Thin
Better to have:
- 50 excellent pages than 200 mediocre ones
- 30 great templates than 100 generic ones
- 10 detailed blog posts than 50 thin ones

### Don't Abandon Your Position
"Simple" is your moat. The moment you add complexity, you become another ClickUp clone.

### Don't Wait for Perfect
Ship the security page even if basic. Ship the blog post even if not perfect. Ship the template even if simple.

**Done > Perfect**
